Pricing FAQ

Can I use MockForge for free?
Yes. The CLI is open source (MIT/Apache-2.0) and always free for local development. The Free cloud tier includes 10K requests/month.
What happens if I exceed my request limit?
Your mocks will continue to respond, but you'll receive a notification to upgrade. We don't cut you off mid-sprint.
Can I change plans at any time?
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.
Do you offer annual pricing?
Yes. Annual plans receive 2 months free. Contact us for details.
